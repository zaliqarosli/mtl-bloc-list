about

MTL BLOC LIST is an informative and playful directory of bouldering gyms in the Greater Montreal area, focused on the story, vibe, and social identity of each gym within the Montreal climbing community.

With climbing's boost in popularity over the last five years, Montreal has seen the opening of over five new gyms within that timeframe, on the island alone. This project aims to provide a fun, interactive way to help those new to the sport or new to Montreal to find the gym whose character and culture they most align with, highlighting the importance of community within the sport.
